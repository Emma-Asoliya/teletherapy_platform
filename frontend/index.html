About Us

At UnMutedMinds, we believe that healing is not one-size-fits-all, it is personal, expressive and rooted in connection.

Tailored to an audience that is not afraid to express themselves, UnMutedMinds provides young people across Africa with a space to find help they might need, and the freedom to be expressive. Whether it is through therapy, conversation or just showing up as yourself, we are here to help you find your voice, and remind you that;
Sometimes breaking down is the first step to rising up
